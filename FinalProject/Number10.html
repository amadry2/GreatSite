Super Mario Bros is the best selling gaming franchise of all time, so of course this juggernaut cannot go without mentioning. The series debuted all the way back in the good ole arcade days, back in 1985, with the title “Mario Bros” featuring a plumber cleaning out hte streets of New York from many different creatures. Little was this known that the sequel to this game would not only be one of the top 10 best selling games and be the shining light of the devastating Video Game Crash of 1983. Ever since then Nintendo has not stopped producing game featuring the Italian plumber, and does not seem to stop anytime soon.
