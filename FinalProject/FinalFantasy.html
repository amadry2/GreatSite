Final Fantasy is one of Square Enix’s most iconic and well-known franchises released in 1987. Ever since then there have been some amazing entries into the series, including the infamous Final Fantasy 7, Final Fantasy 10, and Final Fantasy 12 being some of their best-selling games yet. Final Fantasy 1 has the best sales out of any of Square Enix’s titles, grossing over 11 billion dollars alone. With how many characters are in this series, it’s truly a franchise that will never get old no matter how many games come out.
