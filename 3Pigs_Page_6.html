• Well, the little pig got up at five, and got the turnips before the wolf came (which he did at around six) and said:
  • "Little Pig, are you ready?"
  • The little pig said:
  • "Ready! I have been and come back again, and got a nice potful for dinner."
  • The wolf felt very angry at this, but thought that he would be up to the little pig somehow or other, so he said:
  • "Little pig, Iknow where there is a nice APPLE-TREE."
