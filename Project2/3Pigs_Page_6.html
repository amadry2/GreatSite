• Well, the little pig got up at five, and got the turnips before the wolf came and said:
  • "Little Pig, are you ready?"
  • The little pig said:
  • "Ready! I have got a nice potful for dinner already."
  • The wolf was angry at this, but thought that he was smarter than the little pig, so he said:
  • "Little pig, I know where there is a nice APPLE TREE."
