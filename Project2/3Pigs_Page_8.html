  • The little pig woke up the next morning at four o'clock, and went off for the apples, hoping to get back before the wolf came; but he had more apples to get, and had to climb the tree. Just as he was coming down from it, he saw the wolf coming which frightened him very much. When wolf came up he said:
  • Little pig! Are you here before me? Are they nice apples?"
  • "Yes, very," said the little pig. ""I will throw you down one."
  • While the wolf was going to pick it up, the little pig jumped down and ran HOME.
